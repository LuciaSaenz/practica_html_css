About me

My name is Gordon Shumway, but everybody calls me ALF. I am an alien from the planet Melmac, where I was born on the 28th of Nathinganger, or October 28, 1756 according to Earth's calendar. In 1972 I crash-landed my ship into the garage of the Tanner family, after following an amateur radio signal to Earth. The Tanners have been taking care of me ever since.

Food is love. Food is life

Even though we Melmacians have only four teeth, we LOVE eating. We have very large appetites as we have ten major organs, eight of which are stomachs. Melmacians are omnivorous and are well known for enjoying any kind of food, including things which are inedible for Humans, such as camera film and polyester.
